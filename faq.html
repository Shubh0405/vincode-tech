Faq

Frequently Asked Question

These are some of the Frequently Asked Questions. For more info please contact us here

1. Why should I take part in this contest ?

This is a three-day, full fledged mock placement event which is one of its kind that you do not find every day. From seniors to freshers, everyone can grab something out of this event. If you are a fresher, you will get the understanding about how placements are conducted way ahead of your peers and thus will give a better direction in which you can prepare later when your time comes. If you are someone in second or third year, the placement season will soon be upon you and It can be mighty intimidating sitting in your first interview with a tech giant and not knowing your way through. Thus, you are getting this great opportunity to test your skills and check where you stand compared to others. Not only would we conduct the event, but after everything, we will also provide you with a detailed feedback regarding your performance and what you’re your pluses and minuses, so that you can get a clear picture about what you need to work on.

2. What is the flow of the event?

The event is spanned across three days. On the first day, a coding round will be conducted on hackerearth where you will be required to attend a coding cum aptitude test. The shortlisted candidates will move ahead with a technical round conducted by a panel of experts on the second day. On the third day, industry experts will take on a HR plus technical round for those who get through the second round. Those who clear the third round as well will be declared as the winner(s) of the event.

3.Should I take part if I have not attended a coding contest before?

Yes, definitely. The purpose of this event is to give you a real experience about what goes behind a complete placement procedure. And coding contests are a crucial part of any tech company. So even if you haven’t participated in a coding contest before, it is a real good time for you to experience one and see if you can solve some algorithmic problems in a given time frame or not.

4.What will be the level of difficulty of the questions?

The questions in the coding contest will be of varying difficulty, ranging from easy to hard. So even if you have not solved a hell lot of problems before but are aware about the fundamentals of a programming language and some basic algorithms, you should not find yourself lost. And if you are someone who’s been practicing passionately for a while, then this will be a familiar territory of you.

5.How will the interviews be conducted?

Both the interviews will be conducted over some video calling platform, most likely Google Meet. In the technical round, you will be asked about some algorithmic problems for which you will be required to explain the logic and also give the implementation as and when required. The HR + technical round will have some technical questions along with some general questions to check your personality.
